Lore

Timewalking is reliving the past using a magical hourglass. As the sands within the Hourglass are inverted, a palpable surge of arcane energy envelops the user, transporting them through the veil of time. The world around them shifts and transforms, unveiling an astonishing tableau of a bygone era. They become silent observers, unseen and untouched by the events they witness.
